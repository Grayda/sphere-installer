Ninja Sphere driver installation

This page will help you install or update Ninja Sphere drivers and apps without requiring you to SSH into your device. Please read the instructions carefully, as failure to do so could void the warranty on your Sphere or cause damage

Drivers need to be in the form of a deb file built by the Ninja Sphere CLI. If you don't know what this means, please ask the creator of the driver
